Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

What this site is

cissp.world is a free, open-source study tool for CISSP exam preparation. It is run by an individual, not a company, and does not sell anything — including your data.

What we collect

  • Account data (optional). If you register, we store your email address, display name, and your exam results (scores, ability estimates, per-question answers) in our database, hosted by Supabase. Guest use requires no account; guest progress stays in your own browser.
  • Analytics (only with your consent). If you click "Accept" on the cookie banner, Google Analytics sets cookies and collects usage statistics (pages viewed, exams started and completed, approximate location, device type). If you decline, no analytics cookies are set. You can change your mind by clearing this site's data in your browser — the banner will ask again.
  • Essential storage. The site uses your browser's local storage for things that make it work: your session, your cookie choice, and guest-mode exam history. This is not tracking.

Who else touches the data

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub receives standard web server logs such as IP addresses). Accounts and results live in Supabase. Analytics, if accepted, go to Google Analytics. We share nothing else with anyone.

Your choices

  • Use the site as a guest — no account needed.
  • Decline analytics cookies — everything still works.
  • To delete your account and all associated results, open an issue at github.com/saikatz/cissp-world from the email you registered with, or contact the site owner.

Questions

This project is open source — you can read exactly what the site does at github.com/saikatz/cissp-world.
